Who We Are

Gursale & Company

Founded in 2006, Gursale & Company is a well-established practice of Chartered Accountants based in Karad, Maharashtra. Over two decades, we have grown from a single-partner firm into a full-service CA practice with a dedicated team of professionals.

We serve a diverse clientele ranging from start-ups and SMEs to large corporates, HNIs and NRIs, providing comprehensive solutions in audit, taxation, GST, accounting , and corporate law compliance.

Our philosophy is simple: understand our client's business deeply, provide timely and accurate services, and maintain the highest standards of professional ethics.
