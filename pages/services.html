What We Offer

Expert Services Across All Financial Domains

From routine compliance to complex advisory, our team of qualified Chartered Accountants delivers precision, speed and integrity across every engagement.

Service Category

Audit & Assurance

Company Audits

Statutory audit of companies as per the Companies Act, 2013. We ensure compliance with applicable accounting standards and provide an independent opinion on financial statements.

  • Statutory Audit under Companies Act, 2013
  • Audit of financial statements per Ind AS / AS
  • Audit report and management letter
  • Review of internal controls and processes

Tax Audits

Mandatory tax audit under Section 44AB of the Income Tax Act for businesses and professionals meeting prescribed turnover thresholds.

  • Tax Audit under Sec 44AB of Income Tax Act
  • Form 3CA/3CB and 3CD preparation
  • ICAI standards compliance
  • Timely filing before due dates

Bank Audits

Statutory and concurrent audits of bank branches and concurrent audit of PSU / private sector banks. Empanelled with leading nationalised and private banks.

  • Statutory Branch Audit of Banks
  • Concurrent Audit of Bank Branches
  • Stock audit and NPA verification
  • Revenue audit and credit audit

Stock Audits

Physical verification and valuation of stock on behalf of banks and financial institutions for borrower accounts.

  • Physical stock verification
  • Inventory reconciliation
  • Stock valuation as per bank norms
  • Audit report to bank / institution
Service Category

Direct Tax

Income Tax Compliances

Comprehensive income tax compliance for individuals, HUFs, companies, firms, trusts, and other entities including return filing and advance tax computation.

  • ITR filing for all categories of assesses
  • Advance tax computation and payment
  • Form 15CA/15CB for foreign remittances
  • Income tax planning and advisory

TDS Compliances

End-to-end TDS management including deduction, deposit, quarterly returns and issuance of TDS certificates.

  • TDS deduction and deposit advice
  • Quarterly TDS returns (24Q, 26Q, 27Q, 27EQ)
  • Form 16 / 16A issuance
  • TDS reconciliation and demand resolution

Appellate Litigations

Expert representation before CIT(A), ITAT, High Court, and other tax authorities for assessment and appeal proceedings.

  • Representation before AO, CIT(A), ITAT
  • Assessment and re-assessment proceedings
  • Filing appeals, objections and petitions
  • Settlement Commission proceedings

Tax Structuring

Strategic tax planning and structuring for businesses, M&A transactions, family succession, and investments to achieve optimal tax efficiency.

  • Business tax structure optimisation
  • M&A tax due diligence and structuring
  • Family succession & estate planning
  • Investment and salary structuring
Service Category

GST Services

GST Compliance

Complete GST compliance management including registration, return filing, reconciliation, and annual return.

  • GST registration (regular, composition, TDS/TCS)
  • Monthly / quarterly GST return filing (GSTR-1, 3B)
  • Annual return (GSTR-9) and audit (GSTR-9C)
  • Input tax credit reconciliation

GST Litigations

Representation before GST authorities for notices, audits, assessments, and appellate proceedings at all levels.

  • Response to GST notices and show cause notices
  • Representation before GST Officer, Appellate Authority
  • Advance ruling applications
  • Refund claims and follow-up
Service Category

Accounting Services

Bookkeeping Services

Accurate, timely bookkeeping and accounting services using modern cloud-based software like Tally, QuickBooks, and Zoho Books.

  • Day-to-day accounts entry and maintenance
  • Bank reconciliation and ledger management
  • Payroll processing and salary register
  • Monthly MIS and financial reports

Review of Bookkeeping

Independent review and clean-up of existing books of accounts to ensure accuracy, completeness, and compliance.

  • Verification of books maintained by client's staff
  • Error detection and correction
  • Finalization of accounts for audit
  • Financial statement preparation
Service Category

Project Finance

Project Reports

Detailed project reports (DPR) and CMA data for new businesses, expansions, and bank loan purposes.

  • Detailed Project Report (DPR) preparation
  • CMA data for bank financing
  • Financial projections and feasibility
  • Ratio analysis and break-even calculations

Assistance in Bank Finance

End-to-end assistance in arranging term loans, working capital facilities, and other credit facilities from banks and NBFCs.

  • Loan application preparation and submission
  • Liaison with banks and financial institutions
  • Working capital assessment
  • Renewal of credit facilities
Service Category

Corporate Governance

Company Formation

Complete assistance in incorporation of companies — private limited, public limited, Section 8, OPC, etc.

  • Name approval and DIN/DSC
  • MOA, AOA drafting and filing
  • Certificate of Incorporation
  • Post-incorporation compliances

LLP Formation

Assistance in formation and annual compliance of Limited Liability Partnerships (LLPs) under the LLP Act, 2008.

  • LLP name reservation and DPIN
  • LLP Agreement drafting
  • LLP Incorporation filing (Form FiLLiP)
  • Annual compliance (Form 11, Form 8)

Internal Audits

Risk-based internal audit to assess the adequacy and effectiveness of internal controls, governance, and risk management processes.

  • Internal audit planning and execution
  • Process and system evaluation
  • Fraud risk assessment
  • Internal audit report with recommendations

Arbitration & Managerial

Expert services in arbitration, conciliation, and management consulting for businesses facing disputes or operational challenges.

  • Arbitration and conciliation proceedings
  • Business process consulting
  • MIS reporting and budgeting
  • Financial restructuring advisory
